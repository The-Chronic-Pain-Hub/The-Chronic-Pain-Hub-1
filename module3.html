Module 3

Low Burden Tracking and Bio-Acoustic Micro-Capture

Goal: provide an objective pain truth-check through five-second voice bursts that signal vocal strain.

Back to Module 2 Next: Pain Navigation

Core design

  • One-tap flare button
  • Optional five-second voice memo
  • Optional animation tap
  • No long forms

How it works

The system analyzes how a patient speaks, not what is said. Acoustic feature extraction looks at shimmer, jitter, and vocal strain patterns.

The AI outputs a vocal strain index on a 1.0 to 5.0 scale and tracks patterns across time.

AI layer

  • Extract key acoustic features
  • Identify distress tone
  • Cluster patterns across flare events
  • Generate a simple weekly pattern visualization

Research value

Positioned as a digital biomarker for pain intensity and a strong fit for R21 grant work.

Use case

Creates a low-burden signal when patients are exhausted or struggle to speak in detail.
