How it works

1

Capture

Record a voice sample or upload an existing audio file. Speak naturally for 30+ seconds for best results.

2

Extract

wav2vec2 extracts 768 acoustic features capturing pitch, rhythm, energy, and vocal quality patterns.

3

Analyze

A logistic regression classifier trained on clinical interview data identifies vocal patterns associated with depression.

4

Report

Results show depression probability and confidence score. No audio is stored or retained.
